# Equation Provenance Ledger

Track each major equation/claim with evidence and verification.

## Required fields
- eq_or_claim_id
- statement
- source_type (paper/book/internal)
- source_ref (arXiv id, DOI, book chapter/page)
- derivation_status (derived/reproduced/inferred)
- symbolic_verification (pass/fail/deferred)
- numeric_verification (pass/fail/deferred)
- confidence
- notes

## Rule
No promotion to publishable draft without ledger entry for all key equations/claims.
