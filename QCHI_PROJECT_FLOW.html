QCHI: Research Operating Layer Lifecycle

  • Methodology Straitjacket: A portable operating layer enforcing rigorous, derivation-first workflows for AI agents across multiple hosts (OpenClaw, Cursor, etc.).
  • Analytical First: Mandates symbolic reasoning and mathematical derivation before numerical computation.
  • The Four Horsemen: Strictly enforces validation via unit consistency, limiting cases, asymptotics, and internal cross-checks.
  • Provenance Tagging: Requires explicit tagging of claims (e.g., REPRODUCED_FROM_SOURCE, NEW_EXTENSION) to prevent claim drift and hallucination.
  • Learning Loop: Incorporates a continuous, machine-first log of failure patterns to extract heuristics and improve long-term reliability.
  • Multi-Agent Orchestration: Decomposes complex tasks (like paper reproduction) using specialized roles: Planner, Derivation, Verifier, Referee, and Integrator.
  • LyX Artifact Tracks: Generates separate output tracks for raw notes, systematic plots, and publishable drafts to maintain clarity.
  • Scientific Trust: Acts as a standardized engineering pipeline rather than a chatbot persona, ensuring verifiable and reproducible physics research.
